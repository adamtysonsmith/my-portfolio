Development

JavaScript was the first programming language I learned, and used on a regular basis in my work. When I discovered Node.js and realized that I could create full stack applications in JavaScript very quickly, I fell in love.

Building apps on the MEAN stack has been an amazing journey that I will continue to enjoy.

Web development is my true calling in life, the skill that I enjoy most of all. My obsession began with graphic design. After spending years of teaching myself how to create print and web graphics, I developed a burning desire to take my static designs and bring them to life on the web.

I began learning basic HTML and CSS, and then diving into WordPress.

As my front-end game became stronger, I wanted to do more. I wanted to build fully functional applications that people would enjoy using. My desire evolved into learning about many different programming languages, methodologies, and web architecture before embracing Node.js based web apps.

I love developing on my Macbook Pro using Brackets and Adobe CC Suite.

About Me

I’m Adam - a self taught developer and front-end designer from Atlanta. I have been designing and building stuff since 2011. After learning graphic design to create logos and t-shirt prints, my attention turned to web design and I never stopped learning. I initially wanted to take my designs and bring them to life, but something unexpected happened: Programming became my new love.

I began designing WordPress websites and logos for small businesses for a few years before stumbling upon an opportunity at BKV, an advertising agency here in Atlanta. After about 2 years of working as an SEO Specialist and Data Analyst for BKV, I decided to pursue an entrepreneurial venture unrelated to my love for technology. Although successful, I knew that my talents were not being put to good use. I realized that doing what I love is more important than the money.

What Motivates Me?

I am naturally curious, with an insatiable thirst for knowledge. When I figure out how something works, I will try to push it to the limit until I find a better solution. With JavaScript development, there are seemingly infinite frameworks which is very exciting for me. I enjoy learning new things and thinking about how to leverage them in creative ways.
